# Project: Research of the product group influence in two-sided markets

**Goal**: Conduct a review of scientific and analytical studies examining how different user groups influence one another, and based on this research, determine a method for measuring the influence of women on men in dating services.
**Role**: Product Analyst - Searching for the articles on topic, research.
**Tools**: Analytical thinking, Search skills, Google Sheets.

**Key results**: A detailed research of how the matching function can be used to calculate the influence of one group of users on another, with specific examples.
